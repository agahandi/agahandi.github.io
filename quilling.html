Paper art: quilling

The art of rolling and coiling paper strips

What is quilling?

Quilling art includes rolling and coiling thin paper strips into shapes and designs. The scope of creativity in quilling is vast because it is possible to create different objects such as candle holders, wall accents, greeting cards and jewelry using paper strips. Many believe that quilling began with the invention of paper in ancient Egypt or China and then reached Europe. However, greek people used quilling techniques to roll and shape metal strips into jewelry. Therefore, it seems that quilling was originally the art of coiling metal strings, and over time, the metal was substituted with paper.

Mom Gallery

In 2015, I started learning quilling through Youtube as a hobby, but during the pandemic and lockdown in 2020, I decided to improve this skill and create a small business. Mom gallery was built in 2021 as an online shop named after my mother. At first, only my friends purchased from me, but after eight months, I signed a contract with an accessory shop. Now, I make orders for that shop in addition to online sales.
